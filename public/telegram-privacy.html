Privacy Policy for Celora Wallet Bot

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Celora Wallet through Telegram, we collect:

  • Your Telegram User ID (encrypted)
  • Your Telegram username (if public)
  • Wallet addresses you create or import
  • Transaction history for your wallets
  • Messages you send to the bot for command processing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide wallet management services
  • Process cryptocurrency transactions
  • Authenticate your identity via Telegram
  • Send transaction notifications and alerts
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure key storage using hardware security modules
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • No storage of private keys on our servers (non-custodial)

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To prevent fraud or security threats
  • With your explicit consent

5. Blockchain Transactions

All blockchain transactions are publicly visible on their respective blockchains. While we don't link your identity to wallet addresses, transactions are permanent and cannot be deleted from the blockchain.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting support or using the /delete command in the bot.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

8. Children's Privacy

Celora Wallet is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the bot or via Telegram notifications.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Via Telegram: @Celorawalletbot
  • Email: support@celora.app (if available)
  • Website: https://celora-7b552.web.app
